Use the testnet¶

The testnet is the testing environment for the Terra network. Use this guide to simulate transactions on the testnet.

If this is your first time using Terra Station, follow the Terra Station tutorial.

Get testnet Luna¶

  1. Open Terra Station and connect your wallet.

  2. In the bottom left corner of Terra Station, switch Mainnet to Testnet.

  3. Copy your wallet address. Your wallet address will appear at the top of the Terra station Desktop app near your wallet name. Your wallet address will look like this: terra<random-numbers-and-letters>

  4. Open your browser and navigate to the Terra Faucet .

  5. Paste your Terra address in the Testnet address field, select Luna, and click Send me tokens.

You now have funds in your Testnet wallet. Use the testnet just like you would use the mainnet. Make sure to switch back to the mainnet to make real transactions.
